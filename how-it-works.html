How it Works

There are many ways you can make your Meal Plans on Amiyaa. Let’s get started

Step 1 A

Add any other Meal that you want to plan for besides the default selections

Step 1 B

You can also add your own meal if you want

Step 2 A

Select the date you want to plan the meals for

Step 2 B

You can select upto 7 days

Step 3

Once you are done with this, you can make your meal plans by either of the following ways:

  1. Meal Planning using ingredients available with you Add Your Own

  2. Meal Planning using Amiyaa’s list of ingredients Amiyaa's List

  3. Meal Planning from the options saved in your Library Go Directly to Meal Plan

  4. At any point of time, you can use a combination of any of the above ways to make your Meal Plan too

How to plan Meals from Ingredients available with you at home?

From the Select ingredients screen, please select add your own

Step 1 A

Type the ingredients available with you in the Add Your Own bar

Step 2

Repeat above steps till all required ingredients have been selected. Once done, press on Bottom Tab of CREATE MEAL PLAN

Step 3

Select the meal to plan for by tapping on the + sign in the meal box

Step 4

Click on the bottom tab + Add Dishes from and add dishes from Selected Ingredients

Step 5

Select Ingredients for your chosen meal from the shortlist and press DONE

Step 6

Select from the dish options suggested by Amiyaa or add your own and press DONE

Step 7

If all looks good, press the DONE button on top right corner

Step 8

Select the next meal to plan for as you did in Step 3 and proceed similarly

Step 9

When all meals have been planned for, click on SAVE MEAL PLAN

Step 10

You can add ingredients of your meal plan to your Shopping List by clicking on the Shopping Cart Icon on top right corner of the screen

Step 11

You can download the meal plan into your Photo Gallery by clicking on the three dots arranged vertically on top right corner of the screen

You may also choose to Delete the meal plan from here and start afresh

NOTE: If you exit this module at any time before Saving Meal Plan, you will risk losing all data. You must therefore SAVE MEAL PLAN before exiting

You can access the web for searching the recipes in two ways

Way 1

A. On the home page of library, click on the Search Recipes bar

B. Select Search on Web option from here

A. On the home page of library, Select the Saved From Web folder

B. Press on ‘Search on web’ tab at the bottom of the screen

Once you have selected the ‘Search on Web’ from any of the above ways follow the below steps:

Step 1

Type name of dish or Recipe you are looking for and press Enter

Step 2

Scroll down to review the search findings and select the recipe which you want to Save

Step 3

If you are sure you want to Save this recipe, take a moment and take a screenshot of the image of the dish and save it in your gallery

Step 4

Also do familiarize yourself with the prime ingredients so you are able to add and save the details along with the link

Step 5

Press on ‘Save this link’ tab at the bottom of the screen

Step 6

Give your Recipe a name and add photo that you have saved earlier in the Dish Image

Step 7

Add primary ingredients required for preparing this dish. Add any other details or note if required

Step 8

Press on the ADD TO LIBRARY button and your Link/URL has been saved along with other details manually added by you

“Shop with purpose” and never run out of required ingredients when you are cooking. With all your household requirements listed in one place, you will never have to struggle with multiple lists ever again

The Shopping List Module has two parts: From Meal Plan & Custom List

This is the list of items generated from the Meal Plan when you choose the option to add ingredients to the Shopping List

Step 1

You can click on the tab at bottom of the screen to add items here

Step 2

You can edit the list and add the required quantities of each here

Step 3

Once editing is done, select the Done bar at the bottom to return to Home screen

Step 4

You can remove items not required by you by clicking on the x icon in front of each item. Or you can remove the entire list by selecting Remove all option on the screen

Here you can generate your own custom list, which is in addition to the Meal Plan requirements. This is ideal for your Monthly Household Requirement planning

Step 1

Press on the ‘+’ Add Item bar on the bottom of the screen to get started

Step 2

First Select from the Main categories of Food & Dining Supplies OR Household/Personal Supplies

Step 3

In each of these categories, select any of the sub categories and make your list as per requirement. Once done press “Add to list” button on top right

Step 4

Repeat the above steps for all requirements

Step 5

You can Save and Share the Shopping Lists with others through the respective icons on top right of the screen
